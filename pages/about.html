Our Story

Jackson Hole Expedition Planning grew from a passion for wilderness and a deep love of the Tetons. With years of outfitting experience through Teton Backcountry Rentals, our team saw a need for more personalized and experience-focused trip planning in the region.

Customer Service Philosophy

We aim to provide thoughtful, responsive, and highly personalized planning services. Your adventure should be as effortless as it is unforgettable.

Why Jackson Hole?

Jackson Hole offers access to pristine landscapes, rich wildlife, and unforgettable seasonal experiences—from world-class skiing to serene summer hiking.
