Our Story

JHEP grew from a deep passion for exploration and connection. Founded by long-time guides and local adventurers, our goal is to provide guests with truly insider access to one of the world’s most breathtaking destinations—Jackson Hole.

Customer Philosophy

We believe luxury is not just in where you stay—but how you're cared for. That means personalized communication, curated experiences, and never missing the small touches that turn a trip into a story.
